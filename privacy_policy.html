Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tehri King handles information when you use the Tehri King mobile application.

Information We Collect

Tehri King does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information from users. The app does not require account registration and does not ask for personal details such as name, email address, phone number, location, photos, contacts, or payment information.

Local App Settings

The app may save simple preferences on your device, such as language, sound, and rating prompt status. These settings stay on your device and are not sent to us.

Affiliate Offers and External Links

The splash screen may show affiliate or promotional product images. If you tap an offer, the app opens a third-party website or app in your browser. Tehri King is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of those third-party websites. Please review their privacy policies before sharing any information with them.

Advertising ID and Analytics

Tehri King does not use the Android Advertising ID and does not use analytics SDKs to track users.

Children's Privacy

Tehri King is a card game and is not directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because Tehri King does not collect personal information, there is no personal user data for us to retain or delete. You can clear local app settings by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage from your device settings.

Security

Tehri King does not transmit personal data to our servers. Any external website opened from the app is handled by that third-party service.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on https://sites.google.com/view/tehriking/home.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at tehrikingsupport@gmail.com.
